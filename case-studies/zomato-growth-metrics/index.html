Case Study 05

Data and Experience: Powering Zomato's Growth

A product analytics case study on how food-delivery growth in Tier 1 cities can be improved by connecting user experience signals with funnel metrics.

Analytics
Food Delivery
Tier 1 Cities
Growth Metrics

The problem

In food delivery, growth is shaped by more than acquisition. Users return when discovery is easy, restaurants feel trustworthy, delivery promises are reliable, pricing is clear, and issues are resolved quickly.

The case frames Zomato's Tier 1 city experience through analytics and metrics: where users enter, where they drop, and which experience signals can increase repeat orders.

User needFast, reliable ordering with confidence in restaurant, price, and delivery.
Business needHigher conversion, repeat order frequency, and better retention.

Metric framework

I structured the journey around a practical funnel: app open, search or discovery, restaurant view, cart build, checkout, order placement, delivery tracking, issue handling, rating, and reorder.

01

Discovery metrics

Search success rate, menu engagement, restaurant-card click-through, and time to find a relevant option.

02

Conversion metrics

Restaurant-to-cart conversion, cart abandonment, payment success, and offer usage.

03

Experience metrics

ETA accuracy, cancellation rate, late delivery rate, complaint rate, and post-order rating.

Growth comes from making each decision point feel easier, safer, and more rewarding for the user.

Product opportunities

Discovery

Improve relevance, filter clarity, and restaurant trust signals.

Checkout

Reduce price surprises, payment failures, and last-minute friction.

Delivery

Improve ETA confidence and proactive communication around delays.

Retention

Use reorder prompts, personalized recommendations, and loyalty signals to increase repeat behavior.
