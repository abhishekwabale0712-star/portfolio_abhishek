Case study 02

Nexalink professional networking suite

Roadmap, research, experiments, and go-to-market work that increased engagement by 30% and expanded reach by 15%.

Product Management InternB2B SaaSWebMobi

01

The problem

Professional networking tools need to make capturing, organizing, and following up with connections genuinely easier. Nexalink needed a coherent product direction across its Digital Business Card, Personal CRM, and Smart Scanner experiences.

02

Discovery

I conducted 15+ user interviews and competitor benchmarking for Email Signature and QR Code Generator opportunities, then translated the findings into MVP definitions that balanced user needs, business value, and technical constraints.

Good networking products reduce the work between meeting someone and building a useful relationship.

03

What I did

01

Owned roadmap and vision

Connected the Digital Business Card, Personal CRM, and Smart Scanner into a focused product direction.

02

Validated opportunities

Used interviews, competitive analysis, usability improvements, and A/B testing to guide priorities.

03

Planned market adoption

Crafted and executed a targeted go-to-market strategy for Nexalink.

04

Impact

  • Increased user engagement by 30% through usability improvements and A/B testing.
  • Expanded market reach by 15% within three months.
  • Defined research-backed MVPs for Email Signature and QR Code Generator features.
