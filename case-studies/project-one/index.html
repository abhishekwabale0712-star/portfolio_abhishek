Case study 01

AI-powered flight quotes & booking reliability

A set of product improvements that reduced manual quoting effort by 40%, increased successful bookings by 12%, and made checkout more trustworthy.

Associate Product ManagerVercel AI SDKTravel tech

01

The problem

Flight quoting involved avoidable manual work, while supplier inconsistencies and missing baggage information created uncertainty during booking. Users needed reliable inventory, transparent responses, and more flexible payment options.

Core challengeMake a complex supplier-driven booking flow feel clear and dependable to the traveller.

02

Research and discovery

I examined the workflow bottlenecks behind manual quotes and the points where incomplete baggage or supplier responses caused drop-offs. The opportunity was broader than adding AI: the entire flow needed better inputs, structured responses, and safe fallbacks.

Automation creates value only when the underlying booking response is trustworthy.

03

What I did

01

Shaped the AI quoting workflow

Led development of an AI-powered Flight Quotes feature using the Vercel AI SDK to reduce repetitive manual effort.

02

Improved supplier reliability

Coordinated E-Trav API integrations and introduced structured responses and fallbacks for incomplete booking data.

03

Strengthened checkout and fulfilment

Added EMI payments through JusPay ExpressCheckout and automated PDF ticket generation and delivery.

04

What we shipped

The resulting journey combined AI-assisted quoting, more reliable inventory, clearer baggage visibility, flexible international payment options, and automatic post-booking ticket delivery.

Product visuals can be added when shareable screenshots are available

05

Impact

  • Reduced manual flight-quote effort by 40%.
  • Contributed to a 12% increase in successful bookings.
  • Reduced booking drop-offs by 20% through clearer baggage responses and fallbacks.
  • Contributed to a 15% increase in international bookings through EMI payments.
  • Reduced ticket-PDF support requests by 35%.

06

Product takeaway

AI was one piece of the outcome. The larger product lesson was that automation, supplier reliability, checkout flexibility, and post-booking fulfilment must work together to earn user trust.
