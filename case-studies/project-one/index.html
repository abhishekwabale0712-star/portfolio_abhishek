Case Study 01

AI-powered flight quotes and booking reliability

A travel-tech product improvement initiative that reduced manual quote effort, improved booking success, clarified baggage information, and made post-booking fulfilment more reliable.

AI Product
Travel APIs
Checkout UX
40% manual effort reduction

The problem

Flight quoting and booking had multiple friction points: manual quote creation, supplier response dependencies, unclear baggage information, limited payment flexibility, and avoidable post-booking support requests.

The product challenge was not only to add AI. The journey needed to feel dependable from quote generation through checkout and ticket delivery.

Core issueManual and fragmented booking operations slowed down the user journey.
Product goalMake the flight booking experience faster, clearer, and more reliable.

How I approached it

I looked at the complete booking workflow: how quotes were generated, how supplier responses were handled, where baggage information created uncertainty, and where users needed more flexible payment choices.

The opportunity was to combine AI assistance, supplier integration, better fallbacks, and clearer user-facing information into one stronger product flow.

Automation creates value only when the underlying booking response is trustworthy.

What I did

01

Shaped the AI quoting workflow

Defined requirements for AI-powered flight quotes using Vercel AI SDK to reduce repetitive manual effort.

02

Improved supplier-backed reliability

Coordinated E-Trav supplier API integration and clarified how incomplete responses should be handled.

03

Reduced checkout uncertainty

Improved baggage visibility and supported EMI payment integration through JusPay ExpressCheckout.

04

Improved fulfilment

Helped automate PDF ticket generation and delivery to reduce avoidable support requests.

What was shipped

The final product flow combined AI-assisted quoting, more reliable supplier inventory, better baggage information, EMI payment options, and automated post-booking ticket delivery.

Impact

  • Reduced manual flight-quote effort by 40%.
  • Contributed to a 12% increase in successful bookings.
  • Reduced booking drop-offs by 20% through clearer baggage responses and fallbacks.
  • Contributed to a 15% increase in international bookings through EMI payments.
  • Reduced ticket-PDF support requests by 35%.

What I learned

AI was one part of the outcome. The larger product lesson was that automation, supplier reliability, checkout flexibility, and fulfilment must work together to earn user trust.
